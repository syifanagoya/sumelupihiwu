Who is Adrien Brody:

Adrien Brody is a famous Movie Actor. He was born on April 14, 1973 and his birthplace is Queens, NY. Adrien is also well known as, Dramatic actor who won an Oscar for Best Actor in 2003 for his role in The Pianist, becoming the first actor under the age of thirty to win the award. He also played Steven Schoichet in the 2003 film, Dummy.

Adrien is originated from United States. He won an Academy Award for his role in The Pianist, directed by Roman Polanski.
