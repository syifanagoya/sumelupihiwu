Emily Matson, Esteemed Journalist and Erie News Now Anchor, Passes Away

Emily Matson, a respected television news journalist and anchor for Erie News Now, has tragically passed away. The news of her death has deeply saddened the community of Erie County, Pennsylvania, and beyond.

A Dedicated Reporter and Storyteller

Matson was known for her dedication and passion for journalism. She worked as a general assignment reporter, focusing predominantly on the crime beat. Her commitment to telling the stories that matter and making a difference in Erie was evident in her work.

A Cherished Presence at Erie News Now

Erie News Now, a part of Lilly Broadcasting, mourns the loss of their esteemed colleague. Matson was a beloved figure both within the station and among its viewers. Her contributions to the field of broadcasting in Erie and the US Virgin Islands (USVI) were significant.

Tributes Pour In

Tributes have been pouring in from friends, coworkers, and viewers, expressing their deep sorrow over Matson’s untimely death. Her presence on social media, where she frequently interacted with her followers, will also be greatly missed.

Cause of Death

The cause of Matson’s death has not been officially disclosed as of now.

Remembering Emily Matson

Emily Matson’s passing is a significant loss to the world of journalism and broadcasting. Her dedication, resilience, and passion for storytelling left an indelible mark on her community and her viewers. She will be profoundly missed.
