Tennis star Marcus Willis engaged to girlfriend of five months Jenny Bates

    Earlier this month Marcus Willis told HELLO! that an engagement was on the cards, and it looks like the Wimbledon tennis player couldn't wait any longer. Marcus has proposed to his girlfriend Jenny Bates, and took to Instagram to announce the news.

    "She said Yes!!! Couldn't be happier. #onfire #engaged #willyonarampage," the groom-to-be wrote alongside a snap of himself with Jenny.

    The pretty blonde, who he met during a night out in February, flashed her beautiful diamond rock at the camera as the lovebirds relaxed on a bench.

    Jenny Bates and Marcus Willis met in February

    Marcus, 25, became the new hero of British tennis when he realised his dream of playing at Wimbledon.

    "After trying and failing to play at Wimbledon for six years in a row, I was ready to give up," Marcus told HELLO! magazine.
