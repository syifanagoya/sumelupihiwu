Frank Woodley, originally named Frank Wood, was born on February 29, 1968. He was the youngest child in a family of seven. Woodley grew up in the suburbs of Melbourne, Australia, where his family ran a milk bar in Glen Waverley.

From a young age, he was known by the nickname that would later become his stage name. He legally changed his name to Frank Woodley in 2000.

The Rise of a Comedic Star
