Jairek Robbins Motivational Speaker

Jairek began his career in 2002 working with the Anthony Robbins Foundation where he worked for 2 years. Additionally, he spent his time as a volunteer teacher in Organic farming in the rural farming villages in Uganda.

Jairek worked through the Students Partnership Worldwide Organization. Apart from that, he was a board member of ‘Just Like My Child‘, a non-profit organization.

Later on, Jairek joined Robbins Research International as a Results Coach. He is the founder of the Jairek Robbins Companies in 2010 and also Rapid Results Retreat in 2011. Currently, Jairek hosts a weekly YouTube segment, JRCtv.

Also, Jairek contributes to the Huffington Post blog. In 2013, Jairek did a TEDx talk, ‘A Simple Formula To Inspire the World To Live Their Dreams‘. Jairek authored the book, Live It!: Achieve Success By Living With Purpose.’
