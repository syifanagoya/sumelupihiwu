Kyle Rudolph contract, salary and net worth explored

Kyle Daniel Rudolph, a tight end with the National Football League’s Tampa Bay Buccaneers, was born on November 9, 1989. (NFL). He represented the University of Notre Dame in college football, and the Minnesota Vikings selected him in the second round (43rd overall) of the 2011 NFL Draft. He has received two Pro Bowl selections. He was a member of the New York Giants as well.

Rudolph played high school football for the Panthers while attending Elder High School in Cincinnati, Ohio. He received first-team All-city and All-conference accolades as a junior after catching 30 passes for 400 yards and seven scores. He totaled 37 receptions for 673 yards (18.2 yards per catch) and 11 touchdowns as a senior in 2008, earning him a spot on USA Today’s first team All-American.
