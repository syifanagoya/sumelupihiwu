Kim Kardashian's Curves Dominate 2015 MTV VMAs Red Carpet

Oh hey, Kimye! The Kardashian clan took over the 2015 MTV Video Music Awards at the Microsoft Theater in L.A. on Sunday, Aug. 30, looking gorgeous in an array of curve-hugging outfits.

Pregnant Kim Kardashian West, 34 — wearing a lace-up Balmain dress and with her hair slicked back — posed with her husband, Kanye West, 38. The rapper, who rocked a tan sweater and pushed-up pants with matching Yeezy Boosts, will accept the 2015 Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award, which Beyonce won last year.

Kardashian’s famous sisters Kylie Jenner, 18, and Kourtney Kardashian, 36, posed with their mother, Kardashian family matriarch (and momager!) Kris Jenner, who kept things simple in a black dress and side-swept bangs.
