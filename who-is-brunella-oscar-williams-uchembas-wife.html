Who Is Brunella Oscar, Williams Uchembas Wife?

Brunella rose to the limelight after she got married to Uchemba in November 2020. Her husband is an actor who made his debut in Nollywood in 2000 as a child actor. He is also a comedian, writer, philanthropist, motivational speaker, entrepreneur, and movie producer. The actor has appeared in many movies, including Sugar Rush, Merry Men, and many more.

How Old is Brunella Oscar?

Brunella Oscar is 31 years old. She was born on April 20, 1992, to Apostle and Mrs. Oscar Nwaolisa. The celebrity wife is from Alor in Idemili, South of Anambra State, the southeastern part of Nigeria. According to her state of origin, she is from the Igbo ethnicity and a Nigerian by nationality, though she spent almost all her life in England.

Her father, Mr. Oscar Nwaolisa, is a pastor at the Eternal Life Christian Chapel, located in Manchester, London. What her mother does for a living has remained unknown. She has three siblings, a brother named Junior and two sisters. Christina is the name of the oldest sister in the family, while Deborah, fondly called Debby, is the name of the last sister.

Christina is happily married, while what Junior and Debby do for a living is unknown. They all gained the public’s attention for being the siblings of the celebrity wife, and all made appearances at her wedding. They are seen to be serving the best siblings’ goal as they do not take chances in flaunting each other on their social media handles.

She Has a Degree in Biomedical Sciences

Brunella Oscar has attained a good level of education from universities abroad. She went to Scuola Media Giancarlo Siani and obtained her education at Cowley Language College in the United Kingdom, graduating in 2010.

The celebrity wife obtained a degree in Biomedical Sciences from Anglia Ruskian University in England. Brunella furthered her education at Hull York Medical School, where she obtained a master’s degree in 2018.

Brunella Oscar is a Physician at the Hull York Medical School 

Having attained a great height in the field of medicine from the various schools that she attended, Brunella Oscar is currently practicing. She currently works at Hull York Medical School as a physician. Apart from having a career in the medical field, she is also a philanthropist. The physician is known for improving the well-being of the citizens of Nigeria through the Williams Uchemba Foundation.

The Physician Met Her Husband on Facebook

Brunella Oscar met her husband, Uchemba Williams, on the Facebook platform in November 2016. According to sources, the physician was going through her Facebook page when she stumbled on one of the actor’s motivational videos. In the video, the actor was preaching and advising young people, which instantly sparked something within her.

After 30 to 15 seconds of watching the video, something within her told her that he was her husband. She never knew who he was or heard of him before and slid into his direct message and dropped a message to appreciate his motivational words, thinking that he would not respond.

Brunella Oscar, who had just started her master’s, went to school the next day, announcing to all her friends that she had just met her husband. The news made her friends think that she was out of her mind. However, she thought wrong as the actor gave her a reply and asked her where she was from. From there, they started talking and sparked something within themselves. They usually spent time on video calls, had more fun talking, and felt like they had known each other for years.

According to Brunella Oscar, she revealed they clicked easily, and she didn’t want to scare the actor away by telling him that the Holy Spirit told her he was her husband. She allowed the relationship to grow naturally and waited for God to minister to the actor.

Brunella Oscar and Williams Uchemba Got Married in 2020

Though they did not disclose when they started dating, they may have dated for four years after meeting. The actor proposed and got engaged to Brunella Oscar on October 31, 2020. The overjoyed actor took to his Instagram page to announce his engagement to the love of his life. On November 15, 2020, they tied the knot traditionally at the physician’s hometown, Alor in Idemili South, Anambra State.

The bride dazzled in her stoned flare cream and gold adorned with red coral beads for her first outing. She later changed to a gold-colored gown with a matching gele and purse. Her husband arrived dressed to the nines in an Isi Agu outfit with black trousers and a custom traditional hand fan before changing into a white agbada. The decoration also added to the brightness and lit the moods of people.

The traditional wedding was a huge success and had many actors, actresses, comedians, and dignitaries in attendance. On November 21, 2020, they had their white wedding at Dominion City Church, Lekki Epe expressway. Their wedding was officiated by the senior pastor of the church, Pastor David Ogbueli. Many celebrities like AY, Kate Henshaw, Peter Okoye of Psquare, Falz, Banky W, Rita Dominic, Ini Edo, Timi Dakolo, Ufuomma McDermott, and many other Nigerian celebrities.

The Couple Welcomed Their Daughter in 2022

On February 13, 2022, Williams Uchemba took to his Instagram page to announce the birth of his daughter Chimkamara, Isabella Uchemba. According to him, their daughter arrived on Tuesday, February 8, 2022, in a hospital in England. He accompanied the announcement with a video of his wife’s pregnancy journey. The actor welcomed his daughter and showed his gratitude to her for making him a dad.

Williams Uchemba stated that the whole pregnancy journey and the birth of his daughter felt surreal. He also dedicated a song to his daughter and got his wife a new whip as a push gift. Their daughter is currently growing up under the care of her parents, getting all the love that they could give.
