Benzema Was Born To Parents Of Algerian Descent

Benzema was born in Lyon, France, to Algerian parents who were French citizens.

Before moving to Lyon, where he eventually settled in the 1950s, his grandfather, Da Lakehal Benzema, was a resident of Tigzirt, a village in Algeria’s northern town of At Djellil.

Hafid, Benzema’s father, was born in Tigzirt, while Wahida Djebbara, Benzema’s mother, was born and raised in Lyon; her family was originally from Oran.

Benzema’s father plays professional football for Lyon club AS Monaco. Benzema is the third youngest of nine children and grew up in Bron, an eastern suburb of Lyon, with his eight other siblings.

Gressy and Sabri, two of his younger siblings, are also football players. The first does so on an amateur level with Vaulx-le-Velin in the Tier d’Honneur, France’s sixth division, while the second does so in the youth academy of a club in Bron, the family’s hometown.
