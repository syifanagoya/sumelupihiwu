Justin Evans contract, salary and net worth explored

Justin Evans, a safety with the National Football League’s New Orleans Saints, was born on August 26, 1995. At Texas A&M, he played collegiate football.

Evans was granted entry to the Senior Bowl and made scouts impressed during workouts. Due to his injuries, he was unable to take part in the actual game. He participated in the NFL Combine, but due to a quad injury, he could only finish the bench press.

Evans also took part in Texas A&M’s Pro Day, where he finished most of the combine workouts that he was unable to complete during the combine. He made the decision to skip the three-cone drill and short shuttle.

Most NFL draft experts and observers predicted that Evans would be selected in the second or third round. He was rated the ninth-best safety in the draft by ESPN, the tenth-best safety by Sports Illustrated, and the sixth-best strong safety by DraftScout.com.
