How do you clean a Pier 1 Papasan cushion?

published on May 01, 2024
Answer Remove any dirt or grime from the cushion with a clean cloth, and do your best to thoroughly clean the cushion before putting it in the washing machine. The cushion should be positioned in your bathtub. Pour warm or very warm water into the tub, and fill it about one-third to one-half of the way up, depending on the size and thickness of your cushion. Allow the cushion to soak for a while.
