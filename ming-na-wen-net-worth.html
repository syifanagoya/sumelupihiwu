Early Life and Education of Ming-Na Wen

Ming-Na Wen was born on November 20, 1963, in Coloane, Macau. Her early years were shaped by her family’s journey from Macau to Hong Kong, and eventually to New York City, where they sought better opportunities.

Growing up in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Ming-Na attended Mount Lebanon High School before graduating from Carnegie Mellon University with a degree in Drama. Her early education laid a solid foundation for her illustrious career in acting.

Career Milestones and Achievements

Ming-Na Wen’s career took off with a role on the soap opera As the World Turns. Her breakthrough came with her performance in The Joy Luck Club in 1993.

She went on to play significant roles in films like Street Fighter and Mulan, where she provided the voice for the titular character.

On television, Ming-Na is best known for her roles in ER and Marvel’s Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., where she portrayed Melinda May.
