Genshin Impact Iniquitous Baptist Location &amp; How To Beat

Here is the exact Iniquitous Baptist location in Genshin Impact and how you can beat it easily.

First Published August 9, 2023, 15:54

Genshin Impact 3.6 update brought a brand new world boss named Iniquitous Baptist that you will encounter on your journey in the new areas of Sumeru, Gavireh Lajavard, and Realm of Farakhkert. He can use multiple elements in the battle and can create shields for these elements to reduce the damage. You can beat Iniquitous Baptist to get drops like Evergloom Ring, which is also an ascension material for Baizhu.

If you want to skip the long exploration it might take to find him, you can follow this guide to get his exact location along with the tips to beat him quickly.

Genshin Impact: Iniquitous Baptist Location & How to Beat

Iniquitous Baptist can be found in the Gate of Zulqarnain region of Sumeru, but you will first need to unlock this new area via the Statue of the Seven near Temir Mountains. Once done, you will need to head toward one of the caves marked on the map below; however, we suggest you to go into the cave in Tunigi Hollow to find him easily. 

Keep following the path in front of you, and you will find three elemental lights, and once you go near them, Iniquitous Baptist will spawn to fight you. In order to beat him, we suggest you have a shield character like Zhongli that will protect you from his heavy damage attacks.

Apart from this, you will have to destroy the different shields he will form using a random element to freeze him and make him vulnerable. Thus, you can have a Dendro character like Nahida in your team to utilize elemental reactions like Burning, Quicken, and more to damage the shield no matter what its element is. 

The other two characters can be Raiden Shogun, who will be the main DPS and provide massive damage to Iniquitous Baptist, along with Kazuha, who will help increase Elemental DMG using his skill and burst. 

Apart from this, you will also have to destroy his elemental rings using these characters, and once done, he will freeze, and you can deal massive damage to him to beat him.

That is everything you needed to know about the Iniquitous Baptist location in Genshin Impact, along with the guide to beat him.
