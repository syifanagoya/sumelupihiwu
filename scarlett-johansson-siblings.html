Scarlett Johansson Siblings: Meet Her 5 SIblings: Scarlett Johansson, officially known as Scarlett Ingrid Johansson is an American actress born on November 22, 1984.

She developed a love for acting at a younger age and has been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after actresses.

Johansson first appeared on stage in an off-Broadway play as a child actor and later made her film debut in the fantasy comedy North (1994).

She gained early recognition for her roles in Manny & Lo (1996), The Horse Whisperer (1998), and Ghost World (2001).

Johansson shifted to adult roles in 2003 with her performances in Girl With A Pearl Earring and Lost in Translation, which won her a BAFTA Award for Best Actress.

She was also nominated for Golden Globe Awards for playing a troubled teenager in the drama A Love Song for Bobby Long (2004) and a seductress in the psychological thriller Match Point (2005)

Her other works of this include The Prestige (2006) and the albums Anywhere I Lay My Head (2008) and Break Up (2009), both of which charted on the Billboard 200.

In 2010, Johansson debuted on Broadway in a revival of A View from the Bridge, which won her a Tony Award for Best Featured Actress.

She went ahead to star in the science fiction films Her (2013), Under the Skin (2013), and Lucy (2014).

In 2018 and 2019, Johansson became the highest-paid actress and has been featured multiple times on the Forbes Celebrity 100 list.

For her respective roles in the drama Marriage Story (2019) and the satire Jojo Rabbit (2019), she received two simultaneous Academy Award nominations—Best Actress and Best Supporting Actress.

Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2021. Her films have grossed over $14.3 billion worldwide, making Johansson the highest-grossing box office star of all time.

She has received various accolades, including a Tony Award and a British Academy Film Award, in addition to nominations for two Academy Awards and five Golden Globe Awards.

Johansson has been referred to as one of the world’s most attractive women by various media outlets. She’s a prominent brand endorser and supports several charitable causes.

She supports various charitable organizations including Aid Still Required, Cancer Research UK, Stand Up To Cancer, Too Many Women (which works against breast cancer), and USA Harvest, which provides food for people in need.
